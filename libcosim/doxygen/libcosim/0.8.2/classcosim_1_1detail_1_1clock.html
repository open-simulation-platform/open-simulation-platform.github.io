Detailed Description

A clock type for simulation time points.

This class fulfils the syntactic requirements of the "Clock" concept (https://en.cppreference.com/w/cpp/named_req/Clock), but it does not have any relation to wall-clock time, nor to the logical time of any specific simulation. (The now() method is therefore deleted.) Its sole raison d'Ã¨tre is to be a basis for the definition of time_point.
